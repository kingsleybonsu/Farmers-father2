Why Farmers Father?

Many local farmers lose food because they cannot find buyers on time. Farmers Father helps farmers sell food faster and reduce spoilage.

Supporting local farmers and reducing food waste.

Who Can Use This App?

  • Local Farmers
  • Restaurants
  • Urban Communities
  • Wholesalers

Our Mission

To reduce food waste in villages by connecting farmers directly to buyers using simple technology.
